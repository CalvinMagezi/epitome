Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is a concierge?

    A concierge is someone who is dedicated to providing quality assistance to a client with whatever demand they may have. A concierge also provides solutions in the most resourceful manner possible with access to a host of services and technical expertise.

  • Who is this service most suited to?

    This service is most suited to those that recognise that time is a precious commodity. We provide a service to those that have challenges with fitting trivial tasks into their work schedule thereby incurring higher costs in the long run. Through a wealth of resources and information, we provide value for your money by carefully selecting the best way in which we can overcome these challenges for you.

  • How is our service tailored to your needs?

    EWe add a personal touch in whatever we do for our clients. Understanding your needs and preferences is paramount to our value proposition. We take time to learn who each individual client is, in order to provide a unique service that meets all their demands and augments their quality of living.

  • How do we create value for your money?

    We pride ourselves in availing a range of the best services in Kampala to our clients. Not only do we have access to a host of amazing services, but we also extend these services to our clients at discounted prices. From dining or making orders for delivery at a variety of restaurants in town to maintaining your lawn/compound, we bring you unbeatable prices for high quality services in town.

  • How do we attend to our corporate clients?

    We also cater to institutions and organisations that require a dedicated team of in-house concierges that will meet each and every demand of their employees. From making food deliveries to providing courier services to property management, we redefine professional assistance at an organisational level.
